[heading][color=#e69138]Description[/color][/heading]
Iron Soul is a permadeath-lite death overhaul focused on meaningful stakes, character persistence, and long-form playthroughs.

It combines [b]Dragon Soul Revive[/b], [b]True Death[/b], and [b]Optional Respawn Integration[/b] with [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69267]Respawn - Soulslike Edition[/url], plus per-character data tracking designed to resist tampering.

[b]Important:[/b] Respawn is [b]optional[/b]. Iron Soul still functions as a complete death system without it.

[heading][color=#e69138]What Works Without Respawn[/color][/heading]
[list]
[*]Per-character death tracking and persistent identity
[*]Dragon Soul Revive (when you have Dragon Souls available)
[*]True Death flow and tier-aware death/permadeath presentation
[*]Tiered UI/audio presentation, logging, and persistence systems
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Core Death Lifecycle[/color][/heading]
On death, Iron Soul resolves outcomes in this order:
[list]
[*][b]Dragon Soul Revive[/b] (highest priority): if valid, consumes 1 Dragon Soul and revives you.
[*][b]Cooldown Mode[/b] (when DisableLuckSystem=1 and Optional Respawn Integration is active): no roll, respawn is only allowed when cooldown is ready.
[*][b]Luck Mode[/b] (when DisableLuckSystem=0 and Optional Respawn Integration is active): roll against current Luck; success respawns, failure becomes True Death.
[*][b]True Death[/b] fallback: records death, shows tier-appropriate death/permadeath messaging, and exits the session.
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Optional Respawn Integration[/color][/heading]
[list]
[*]Respawn support is [b]optional[/b] and controlled by config + quest availability.
[*]If Respawn integration is missing, disabled, or not running, Iron Soul [b]fails closed[/b] and routes to True Death behavior instead of partial/inconsistent respawn behavior.
[*]When integration is active, Iron Soul uses respawn-window protections, respawn messaging, and post-respawn state recovery.
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Luck Mode vs Cooldown Mode[/color][/heading]
Both modes are tied to [b]Optional Respawn Integration[/b].

[b]Luck Mode[/b] ([b]DisableLuckSystem=0[/b])
[list]
[*]Each eligible death rolls against your current Luck value.
[*]Luck resets on respawn and on True Death.
[*]Luck regenerates from 0 to max over [b]60 minutes real time[/b].
[*]Menu time does not advance Luck/Cooldown progression.
[/list]

[b]Cooldown Mode[/b] ([b]DisableLuckSystem=1[/b])
[list]
[*]No luck roll is performed.
[*]Respawn is only available when the [b]60 minute cooldown[/b] is fully recovered.
[*]Cooldown resets on respawn and on True Death.
[*]Uses the same real-time/menu-safe timing model as Luck Mode.
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Progression and Death Caps[/color][/heading]
[list]
[*]Default [b]Iron[/b] cap is 10 deaths.
[*][b]Defiant[/b] cap is 100 deaths (when Defiant is unlocked and activated).
[*][b]CHIM[/b] tier removes practical cap limits (optional via INI).
[/list]

[b]Feat and Tier Highlights[/b]
[list]
[*]Defiant unlock condition: absorb at least 1 Dragon Soul with fewer than 10 deaths.
[*]Silver: 10 Dragon Souls obtained.
[*]Gold: 20 Dragon Souls obtained.
[*]Ebon: defeat Alduin or Harkon.
[*]Platinum: defeat Miraak or Molag Bal (Vigilant-supported path).
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Persistence and Integrity[/color][/heading]
[list]
[*]All progression is tracked [b]per character[/b] via a persistent GUID identity model.
[*]Native datastore uses main + mirror files, integrity checks, and self-heal behavior.
[*]Co-save backups are maintained for key per-character values.
[*]Optional anti-cheat handling for abnormal Dragon Soul jumps.
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Skyrim Character Sheet Compatibility (Optional)[/color][/heading]
Iron Soul includes optional support for [b][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56069]Skyrim Character Sheet[/url][/b].

When enabled, total deaths are mirrored to actor value [b]DEPRECATED05[/b] for Character Sheet display compatibility.

[heading][color=#e69138]Configuration Highlights[/color][/heading]
Config path: [b]Data/SKSE/Plugins/IronSoul.ini[/b]

[list]
[*][b]DisableRespawn[/b]: turn Optional Respawn Integration on/off.
[*][b]DisableLuckSystem[/b]: switch between Luck Mode (0) and Cooldown Mode (1).
[*][b]DisableDragonSoulRevive[/b]: disable Dragon Soul Revive.
[*][b]DisableDeathMessage[/b], [b]DisableRespawnMessage[/b], [b]LoadNotificationMode[/b], [b]LuckRollMessageMode[/b]: control message/UI behavior.
[*][b]DisableSoulFeats[/b], [b]DisableSoulBonus[/b], [b]DisableSoulFatigue[/b], [b]DisableDefiantFeat[/b]: control progression and stat-modifier behavior.
[*][b]DisableDragonSoulAnticheat[/b]: disables anti-cheat filtering for Dragon Soul gain deltas.
[*][b]CHIM[/b]: enables CHIM transition path.
[*][b]EnableCharacterSheetCompatibility[/b]: mirrors death count to DEPRECATED05.
[*][b]UninstallMode[/b]: safe disable/uninstall flow.
[/list]

[heading][color=#e69138]Uninstallation[/color][/heading]
[list]
[*]Set [b]UninstallMode=1[/b] in IronSoul.ini.
[*]Load your save and wait for the uninstall confirmation.
[*]Save, then remove the mod (or leave it installed in disabled state).
[/list]
